Signing Up and Logging In

  • How do I sign-up?

    You can sign up for a MonkeyTalk membership by clicking on the Sign Up tab in the navigation bar. Then simply fill out all the required forms and click the submit button.

  • My citizenship is not listed in the sign up page!

    In this case, select the '--Other--' option in the 'Citizenship:' drop down menu. A text field will appear in which you can type in your citizenship.

  • Why do I have to type in that funny text at the end?

    This is to ensure that you are a human! Online spammers make a habit of writing automated scripts to sign up for as many differents sites as they can. This is one of the ways they spread spam. By typing in the verification text, you are letting us know that you are a human and not some script written by a spammer!
